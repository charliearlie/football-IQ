ðŸ’¡ Cheat Sheet: How to work it out!

MEAN (The Average)
Add all the numbers up, then divide by how many numbers there are.
MEDIAN (The Middle)
Put the numbers in order from smallest to largest. Find the one in the middle.
MODE (The Most)
The number that appears the most times.
RANGE (The Difference)
Take the biggest number and subtract the smallest number.
