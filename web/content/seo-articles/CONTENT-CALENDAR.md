## Article Templates

### Monday — Weekend Recap Quiz
- **Slug pattern:** `YYYY-MM-DD-weekend-recap-quiz`
- **Structure:** Intro paragraph → 10 questions about the weekend's results → Answers section → CTA to play daily puzzles
- **Internal links:** Topical Quiz + at least 1 other game mode
- **Example title:** "Weekend Recap Quiz: 10 Questions From This Weekend's Football"

### Wednesday — "Can You Name..." Listicle
- **Slug pattern:** `YYYY-MM-DD-can-you-name-[topic]`
- **Structure:** Challenge intro → Numbered list → Fun facts interspersed → Answers hidden/below → CTA
- **Internal links:** Connections (pattern-matching) + Career Path (player knowledge)
- **Example titles:**
  - "Can You Name Every Champions League Winner Since 1993?"
  - "Can You Name the Top 10 Premier League Scorers of All Time?"
  - "Can You Name Every Player to Score a World Cup Final Hat-Trick?"

### Friday — Trivia Deep Dive
- **Slug pattern:** `YYYY-MM-DD-[descriptive-topic]`
- **Structure:** Long-form narrative → Historical context → Statistical nuggets → Quiz element → CTA
- **Internal links:** Timeline (chronological) + Transfer Guess (deals)
- **Example titles:**
  - "The 20 Most Expensive Transfers That Flopped"
  - "Football's Forgotten Cup Finals: 10 Matches History Overlooked"
  - "Record Breakers: Every Major Football Record Set in 2025-26"

## SEO Checklist (Every Article)

- [ ] meta_title: keyword-first, under 60 characters
- [ ] meta_description: includes CTA verb, under 160 characters
- [ ] H1 contains primary keyword
- [ ] At least 2 internal links to /play/{game-mode}
- [ ] At least 1 link to another blog article (once we have >3 published)
- [ ] App download CTA at the bottom
- [ ] Slug format: `YYYY-MM-DD-descriptive-keyword-slug`
- [ ] Excerpt provided (shown on blog index)
- [ ] Content is 800+ words (1500+ for deep dives)
